normal gait;head: stays upright and aligned above a stable trunk; hand: both hands move naturally with the walking rhythm; arm: arms swing bilaterally with regular alternating steps; hip: pelvis remains stable and advances along a mostly straight path; leg: legs alternate with regular stride and adequate lift; foot: feet clear the ground with natural heel strike and steady placement.
mild gait impairment;head: remains mostly controlled with slight trunk stiffness or mild forward lean; hand: hands continue to move with the gait but the swing is slightly reduced; arm: arm swing is present with mild asymmetry or stiffness; hip: pelvis keeps forward progression with mild side drift or stiffness; leg: legs alternate independently with mildly shortened steps; foot: foot lift is mildly reduced but stepping remains stable.
moderate gait impairment;head: head and trunk show clear forward lean, stiffness, or instability; hand: hands move less consistently and may stay closer to the body; arm: arm swing is clearly reduced, stiff, or asymmetric; hip: pelvis wanders, backtracks, or bends into a curved path with reduced stability; leg: legs take shortened shuffling steps with visible asymmetry; foot: foot clearance is reduced and placement looks less stable.
severe gait impairment;head: head and trunk rotate or lean with poor postural control; hand: hands often stay close to the body with minimal functional swing; arm: arm swing is strongly reduced, stiff, or asymmetric; hip: pelvis loops, turns in place, or backtracks instead of moving straight forward; leg: legs rotate around a small area or take very short shuffling steps; foot: feet show poor clearance with weak forward progression and unstable placement.
